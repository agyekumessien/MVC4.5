Who We Are

We provide academic, career and personal counseling to students. We have flexible timetables to suit students & scheduled appointments for student counseling needs. We also offer classes & workshops throughout the semester to meet the diverse interests and needs of students. Durban International College has a range of student services including support services. Student services aims to assist students to successfully adjust to living in Australia and to support Academic Progress.

Contact: - info@durban.sa.edu.au for further information.
